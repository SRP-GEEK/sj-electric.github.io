RES

Residential Projects

Complete electrical solutions for homes, apartments, and residential complexes. From basic wiring to smart home automation, we supply quality materials that ensure safety, efficiency, and modern convenience for homeowners and builders.


What We Supply:
  • House wiring cables and accessories
  • Switches, sockets, and modular plates

  • LED lighting and fixtures

COM

Commercial Buildings

Powering offices, retail spaces, hotels, hospitals, and commercial establishments with reliable electrical infrastructure. We provide comprehensive materials for HVAC integration, backup power, energy management, and code-compliant installations.


What We Supply:
  • Commercial-grade wiring systems
  • Power distribution panels

  • Energy-efficient LED solutions
  • UPS and backup power systems
  • Building management components
IND

Heavy-duty electrical solutions for manufacturing plants, warehouses, processing facilities, and industrial complexes. We supply ruggedized equipment designed to withstand demanding environments and ensure continuous operations.
