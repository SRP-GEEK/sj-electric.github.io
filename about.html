Our Story

SJ Enterprises is a family-run electrical supply business with over 50 years of legacy, built across four generations.

Founded in 1975, we began as a local electrical accessories supplier. Today, we support large-scale industrial, commercial and infrastructure projects with reliable products, fair pricing and long-term partnerships.

Our strength lies in product knowledge, BOQ-based quotations and dependable supply — making us a preferred B2B partner.
