Our Legacy of Excellence

Four generations, 50+ years of trust, and countless successful projects. Discover the SJ Enterprises story.

Since 1975

SJ Enterprises was established in 1975 with a simple mission: to provide quality electrical accessories and project materials backed by integrity and exceptional service. What began as a modest venture has grown into a trusted partner for contractors, builders, and industries across the region.

Today, we carry forward a legacy that spans over 50 years and four generations. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction remains as strong as it was on day one. We've witnessed the evolution of the electrical industry firsthand, adapting and growing while staying true to our core values.
