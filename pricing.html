Transparent, BOQ-Based Pricing

Every project is unique. Our customized quotations ensure you get the best value for your specific requirements.

Why We Don't Display Fixed Prices

Unlike consumer products, electrical project materials involve numerous variables - quantities, specifications, brands, delivery timelines, and project-specific requirements. Fixed pricing doesn't account for these nuances and can lead to either overcharging or undervaluing your needs.

That's why we use a Bill of Quantities (BOQ) based approach. This method ensures you receive a detailed, accurate quotation tailored to your exact project specifications, giving you complete transparency and the best possible value.

Our 4-Step Quotation Process

1

Submit Your BOQ

Send us your project's Bill of Quantities via WhatsApp, email, or through our contact form. If you don't have a formal BOQ, share your requirements and we'll help you create one. Include details like quantities, specifications, brands (if any preference), and delivery timeline.

2

We Analyze & Quote

Our experienced team reviews your BOQ, confirms specifications, and prepares a detailed quotation. We itemize each component with clear descriptions, quantities, unit prices, and total costs. Typically delivered within 24-48 hours depending on project complexity.

3

Discussion & Refinement

We walk you through the quotation, answer questions, and make adjustments based on your feedback. Need to change specifications? Want to explore alternative brands? Looking for cost optimization? We refine the quote until it perfectly matches your needs.

4

Approval & Delivery

Once you approve the quotation, we confirm your order, arrange procurement, and schedule delivery. We keep you updated throughout the process and ensure materials arrive on time and in perfect condition. Post-delivery support is always available.

How to Prepare Your BOQ (Bill of Quantities)

Don't worry if you're not familiar with creating a BOQ - we're here to help! Here's what to include:

📝 Item Descriptions

List each electrical component you need (e.g., "Circuit Breakers", "Power Cables", "LED Lights")

🔢 Quantities

Specify how many units of each item you need, with appropriate units (pieces, meters, sets, etc.)

⚙️ Specifications

Include technical specs like voltage ratings, current capacity, cable sizes, power ratings, etc.

🏷️ Brand Preferences (Optional)

If you have preferred brands or manufacturers, mention them. Otherwise, we'll recommend quality options.

📅 Timeline

Let us know your delivery deadline so we can plan procurement and logistics accordingly.

Don't have a formal BOQ? No problem! Just share your project details and requirements, and our team will help you create one.
